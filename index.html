Salty Recipes

Onigiri (Rice Balls)

Onigiri, or rice balls, are a popular Japanese snack often seen in anime. They are made with seasoned rice and can be filled with a variety of ingredients like tuna, salmon, or pickled plums.

Ramen

Ramen is a beloved Japanese noodle soup with a rich broth, noodles, and various toppings such as sliced pork, boiled eggs, and green onions. It's a staple in many anime series.

Sweet Recipes

Taiyaki

Taiyaki is a fish-shaped cake filled with sweet red bean paste, custard, or chocolate. It's a common street food treat in Japan and often appears in anime.

Dango

Dango are sweet rice dumplings often served on a skewer and drizzled with sweet soy sauce. They are frequently depicted in anime festivals.
